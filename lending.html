What we do

The financing side,
handled alongside the rest

Lending is one of the most important — and most confusing — parts of buying a home. The right loan can change your monthly payment, your timeline, and your options. We help you understand what's available and what makes sense for your situation.

Because Front Door handles escrow, buying, and selling under the same roof, we can coordinate the lending process alongside the rest of your transaction — which means fewer gaps, fewer surprises, and a smoother path to close.

We work with buyers at every stage — from those just beginning to explore their options to those who are ready to move quickly in a competitive market.

What we help with

Lending support that fits your goals

Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor, understanding your financing options shouldn't require a finance degree. We translate the details and connect you with the right resources.

  • Guidance on loan types and what's right for your situation
  • Coordination with lenders, escrow, and all parties involved
  • Support for first-time buyers, move-up buyers, and investors

  • Based in El Segundo — local South Bay lending support
The process

From options to approval

Lending has a lot of steps. We walk you through each one and keep everything coordinated.

01

Understand your options

We start with your goals — budget, timeline, and priorities — and help you understand which loan types and programs make sense for your situation.

02

Connect with the right lender

We connect you with lenders suited to your needs, whether that's a conventional loan, FHA, VA, jumbo, or another program entirely.

03

Move through pre-approval

We help you understand what documents are needed and what to expect — so pre-approval moves quickly and cleanly.

04

Coordinate with escrow

Because we handle escrow in-house, we keep the lending and escrow timelines aligned — reducing delays and keeping your transaction on track.

05

Close with confidence

We confirm all financing conditions are cleared and coordinate with all parties so your loan funds on time and closing goes smoothly.
